About DesiDukaan

Founded in 2020, DesiDukaan is a celebration of India's diverse culture and rich heritage. We bring together authentic handcrafted products from artisans across the country, providing them with a platform to showcase their craftsmanship to the world.

Our mission is to preserve traditional Indian arts while making them accessible to modern consumers. Each product in our collection tells a story of India's cultural legacy.
